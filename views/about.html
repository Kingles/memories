Our Story

m.emori.es is built from organic and top quality ingredients in Portland, Oregon. It was conceived and developed by a group of friends to solve basic planning and communication issues during group trips. The first version of m.emori.es premiered at HackGT in Atlanta, Georgia.

Core Principles

Dignity We do not subject our users (and never will) to advertisements. In addition, we have no interest and will never perform data experiments on users or mine data.

Privacy Data belongs to our users 100%. The m.emori.es team works for its user base and provides quality software to best manage your data. We will never share your data with third parties

Quality We're our own customer and we take our app experience seriously. In addition, we are not investment focused but rather product focused.

Security Since m.emori.es is not focused on advertisers, social networks or hype in-gneral, it allows us to focus on securing data and making sure your most candid moments always remain private between your friends.

Protected Cloud License

Your data is protected for life and your data is guaranteed to always be privately owned by you.
